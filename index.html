Why use Go Green?

Discovering valid sources for sustainable food shopping in your area, including organic resturants and grocery stores that have locally growned or produced product, can be incredibly difficult. Go Green Food Sourcing is a website to help with this, by providing links to reputable sustainable food shopping options.

Impact!

Where can this have lasting effects?

Purchasing from grocery stores that have locally grown or produced products supports the local farms and agriculture economy, which reduces food waste and contributes to a healthier community.

Why purchase organic or vegetarian?

Dining in organic restaurants supports sustainable farming that's healthier for our environment. Having accessible vegetarian options reduces carbon emissions created by the production of meat products.

What can I do?

Start small! Find acessible ways to incorporate healthier local food options into your daily habits and diet.
